Our Story

Founders, Lawrence Helfant and Duncan Abdelnour set out to create the first company, CrowdSync Technology. The mission of CrowdSync started simple, to create unforgettable event experiences through our hardware technology products. Bootstrapped from the beginning, CrowdSync began winning customers, lighting up events, and scaling both quality and in quantity.

Upon recruiting a third partner in Christopher Foote, our event technology company suddenly paused due to the pandemic. Questioning our next steps, we pivoted to build CrowdHealth. CrowdHealth started delivering millions of masks to those in right away and began to expand into multiple lines of healthcare products, primarily covid tests and testing as a service.

Combining our strengths in CrowdSync, event technology and CrowdHealth, healthcare supplies we self-funded the development of our first software, CrowdPass. Similar to the high demand for medical products, the need for a digital software to verify, organize, monitor health records, specifically being covid-vaccination proof.

Understanding the urgent desire and demand for events to come back safely, CrowdPass became a pillar for thousands of event organizers and more than a million attendees to upload vaccine or test results for our software to validate the attendee is vaccinated. Thereafter, leveraging the CrowdPass QR Code to enter the event.

Now the three partners, three businesses, 18 people across all companies, we are exited it’s now time to introduce CrowdVentures, a New York City Venture Studio.
